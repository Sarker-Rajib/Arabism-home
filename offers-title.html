Desert trip Kashtat

Region : Riyadh

Code : DES-KASHTAT-01

City : Al Muzahmyiah

Duration : 10 hrs

Description

In this desert trip you will be guided to one of the unique desert spots  that you will find the opportunity to make you live an amazing experience and delightful moments.
You well be surprised with what desert can be a place  for entertainment and nice time. It is the best destination for escaping from the city crowd, for you, your family, friends or partners to a peaceful and clear natural spot to enjoy  the magnificently beautiful scenery of the desert.
During this trip, you will have services that will reflect a real life experience of adventures.
Arabism Land will make your journey an unforgettable rich experience.




Instructions

Details about the event, meeting point location link shall be shared by the Tour Leader via a text message one day prior the event.

Tours/trips will start  at the time mentioned on the schedule. It is recommended that you arrive at the meeting point at least 10-15 minutes before the start of the tour/trip.

All our team members including tour guides will provide you with necessary instructions on the trip safety, and will offer support and deliver best practices for pleasant experience.




We are not responsible for special diets.

Wear outdoor clothing depending on the weather Take shoes that are good for walking.

Please leave your valuables or sensitive goods home/hotel.
